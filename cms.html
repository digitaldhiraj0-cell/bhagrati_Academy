Yes. The existing frontend stays responsive and optimized while the content becomes fully dynamic.

Yes. Headings, paragraphs, buttons, images, videos, notices, teacher profiles, admissions details, footer content, and more.

Yes. Published updates are fetched dynamically from the backend and reflected on the frontend.

Yes. The system includes JWT authentication, protected routes, validation, rate limiting, and security best practices.
